SOBRE NOSOTROS

Orthocare’s team has been working with neurosurgeons, orthopedic doctors, health-care professionals and medical institutions since 2023. We partnered with some of the top companies in order to provide surgeons, access to the best surgical equipment including: implants for trauma, spine, reconstruction neurosurgery, maxillofacial and biologics. We also provide technician services for local surgeons, billing and coding services for orthopedic surgery and provide an institutional support for medical billing. We look forward to a bright future and to provide the best quality of service to our clients and partners in Puerto Rico.

HELIX3-CP Collagen Powder
Maintain an optimal wound environment to promote moist healing of draining wounds with HELIX3-CP® Collagen Powder. The 100% Type 1 bovine native collagen particles retain their native helical structure, increasing bioavailability to deliver the benefits of collagen to the wound immediately and enhancing the vital scaffolding process for granulation tissue formation. HELIX3-CP absorbs 40-60 times its weight in fluid and contributes up to 10 times more denatured collagen protein than competing products. HELIX3-CP contains no additives, synthetics, or fillers and is effective in all wound phases.

AMERX Wound Care Kit - Collagen Matrix
Provide everything you need for your patients to perform proper dressing changes at home. AMERX Collagen Matrix Wound Care Kits are available for 30-Day or 15-Day treatment lengths based on your patient’s individual wound care needs.
HELIX3-CG Collagen Gel
Deliver Type 1 native tropocollagen directly to your hard-to-heal wounds with HELIX3-CG Collagen Gel. Formulated to deliver the healing benefits of collagen with fewer fillers than the competition, HELIX3-CG provides an industry-leading 90% of active ingredients with each application. Whether you are looking to resolve dry to moderate exudating wounds, wounds with tunneling, undermining or hard-to-access areas, HELIX3-CG is your solution for advanced wound care.

C-Armor: Disposable C-Arm Drapes for Surgical Procedures

The TIDI C-Armor C-Arm Drape can help to safeguard patients from the risk of contamination during surgical procedures involving C-arm imaging systems. By helping to ensure sterility is maintained while facilitating workflow, the C-Armor C-Arm Drape is a patented, user-friendly solution that can be ideal for surgeries that require multiple C-arm swings. Its innovative design allows for quick and efficient setup, minimizing disruptions during the procedure. Additionally, the C-Armor C-Arm Drape contributes to a safer operating environment, promoting overall patient well-being and satisfaction.

C-Armor: Cortinas desechables C-Arm para procedimientos quirúrgicos

Las cortinas médicas TIDI C-Armor C-Arm puede ayudar a proteger a los pacientes del riesgo de contaminación durante procedimientos quirúrgicos que involucran sistemas de imágenes de arco en C. Al ayudar a garantizar que se mantenga la esterilidad y al mismo tiempo facilitar el flujo de trabajo, C-Armor C-Arm es una solución patentada y fácil de usar que puede ser ideal para cirugías que requieren múltiples oscilaciones del arco en C. Su diseño innovador permite una configuración rápida y eficiente, minimizando las interrupciones durante el procedimiento. Además, el C-Armor C-Arm Drape contribuye a un entorno operativo más seguro, promoviendo el bienestar y la satisfacción general del paciente.
